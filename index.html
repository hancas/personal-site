Push, Often.

Below are select samples of recent works. Each one was made with the intent of pushing creative boundaries and improving the quality of each subsequent project.

haenyo

haenyo is a colorful Korean pop-up food venture started by Irvin Seo and Collin Morstein. We developed the haenyo identity to be a flexible visual system so that we could incorporate some of the flavor of the restaurants that graciously hosted us while still maintaining a recognizable presence. The pop-up has executed over 50 dinners since its inception in 2017.

ScratchMade

Collin Morstein and Irvin Seo expanded their skills in the kitchen beyond their pop-up dinners by creating a meal service with a plethora of options, all made from scratch. We created an identity and design system that drew inspiration from antique Italian and Russian recipe cards. We set out to create something playful and injected with some humor.

Future Scopes: ScratchMade Website, fall 2018

Power Slam Wrestling

Gnarwhal Studios (creators of Humans vs. Zombies and Slap45) that I worked on with Christopher J Chester. Together we designed and illustrated the identity, cards, and board. The visuals were heavily influenced by prime 80's wrestling androgyny and glam. Watch a beta test of the gameplay here.

Urban Teachers

For this project, I built all of the the front-end development with the goal of maintaining the integrity of the original flats and assets (designed by Christopher) This website was built from scratch, utilizing relative units instead of solid pixels, with CSS grids to keep the site light and responsive to emphasize the clean design.

Pilon

As a headless API-first e-commerce product, it was important that Pilon’s website appealed to advanced developers in the field. With this in mind, the design needed to be based around interactive visual elements, like the animated gradient background, to emphasize lightweight technology that’s easy to use.

Foodshed

Foodshed (founded by chef Spike Gjerde) is a Baltimore based restaurant group, consisting of: Artifact Coffee, Woodberry Kitchen, Parts & Labor, Grand Cru, and Bird in Hand. I worked in-house and directly with chefs and managers to design all material for events, menus, social presence, and retail packing within individual restaurant brand guidelines.
